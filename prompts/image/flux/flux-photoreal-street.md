# FLUX Photoreal Street Scene

适用模型：FLUX.1、FLUX.2、Z-Image、LongCat-Image

## Prompt

```text
A photorealistic evening street scene in a modern Chinese city after light rain. Wet pavement reflects warm shop lights and soft traffic colors. A young man in a dark coat walks across the frame holding a transparent umbrella, seen from a slightly low angle. The background is softly blurred with believable pedestrians and storefronts, cinematic realism, natural motion feel, 35mm street photography, subtle film grain, balanced contrast, no readable signs.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
fake text, distorted signs, crowded chaos, anime style, plastic skin, over-sharpened details, low quality, watermark, logo, oversaturated neon
```

## Notes

- 城市夜景容易变赛博朋克，想写实就要强调 realistic、natural、subtle。
