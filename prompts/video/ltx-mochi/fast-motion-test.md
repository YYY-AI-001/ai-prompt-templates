# LTX / Mochi Fast Motion Test

适用模型：LTX-Video、Mochi、轻量开源视频模型

## Prompt

```text
A 4-second dynamic shot of a red sports bicycle moving along a quiet riverside road. The camera tracks beside the bicycle at the same speed, keeping the frame stable. Morning sunlight creates long shadows on the pavement, trees pass softly in the background, realistic wheel rotation, smooth lateral motion, no text, no logo.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
warped wheels, broken bicycle frame, unstable camera, motion smear, flickering, low quality, extra riders, watermark, subtitles
```

## Notes

- 轻量视频模型适合做运动测试：车辆、自行车、镜头跟拍、光影变化。
