# FramePack Character Loop

适用模型：FramePack、低显存长视频 / 续写视频模型、I2V 模型

## Prompt

```text
A young painter sits beside a window in a quiet studio, slowly mixing blue paint on a wooden palette. She lifts the brush, pauses for a moment, then adds a small stroke to a canvas just outside the camera's focus. The motion is calm and repeatable, with subtle hand movement, breathing, and soft fabric movement. Warm afternoon light, realistic interior, stable character identity, no dialogue, no text.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
identity drift, changing clothes, broken hand motion, duplicated brush, flickering canvas, sudden scene cut, subtitles, watermark, logo
```

## Notes

- FramePack / 续写类视频适合“可持续的小动作”，不要一开始就写复杂剧情。
- 如果用图生视频，参考图要明确人物服装、空间关系和手部位置。
