# LTX / Mochi Anime Style Short

适用模型：Mochi、LTX-Video、Wan 动画风格

## Prompt

```text
A 4-second anime-style shot of a young girl standing on a quiet train platform at sunset. Her short hair and school uniform move gently in the wind. A train slowly passes behind her, creating soft motion blur. Warm orange sunlight, calm nostalgic mood, clean anime film style, stable camera, no text, no subtitles.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
face distortion, changing outfit, extra people, chaotic background, flickering, warped train, low quality, watermark, subtitles, logo
```

## Notes

- 动画风格也要保持动作简单，避免多个角色复杂互动。
