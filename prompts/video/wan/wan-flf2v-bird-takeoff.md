# Wan First-Last-Frame Takeoff

适用模型：Wan FLF2V / 首尾帧视频模型

## Prompt

```text
CG animation style, a small blue bird takes off from the ground and flies upward into a bright sky. The bird keeps the same feather pattern and body shape from the first frame to the last frame. The camera follows the bird upward from a close-up low-angle perspective, showing the movement from standing to flight. Bright sunlight, soft clouds, smooth wing motion, cheerful atmosphere.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
different bird, changing feather color, broken wings, frozen motion, chaotic camera, distorted body, flickering, subtitles, watermark, logo
```

## Notes

- 首尾帧模式适合“起点和终点明确”的镜头。
- Wan 官方资料建议 FLF2V 任务可使用中文 prompt；如果中文效果更好，可将本提示词翻译为中文。
