# SD3.5 Typography Ad

适用模型：Stable Diffusion 3.5 Large / Medium、其他支持文字生成的新一代图像模型

## Prompt

```text
A premium minimalist advertisement for a compact AI camera named "Luma One". The camera sits on a matte white pedestal, front-facing at a slight three-quarter angle, with brushed aluminum details and a small black lens. The background is warm light gray with soft studio shadows. Add one short headline at the top in clean sans-serif typography: "Capture the Invisible". Add a small product label below the camera: "Luma One". Balanced commercial composition, luxury product photography, crisp edges, natural reflections, high-end catalog style.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
misspelled text, extra random letters, duplicated product name, cluttered layout, cheap plastic look, distorted lens, watermark, logo, low resolution, blurry
```

## Notes

- SD3.5 对自然语言和文字有较好跟随能力，短句文字最好用英文双引号包起来。
- 文字越短越容易稳定；复杂中文排版建议用 Qwen-Image 或后期排版。
