# FLUX Product Material Study

## Prompt

```text
A material study product render of a compact AI hardware device on a clean white desk. The device has a matte ceramic body, brushed aluminum side rails, a small soft-glowing status light, and a gently curved top surface. Morning sunlight passes through sheer curtains, creating soft shadows. Macro product photography, realistic material response, precise edges, minimal composition, premium industrial design, no logo, no text.
```
