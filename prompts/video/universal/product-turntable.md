# Universal Product Turntable Video

适用模型：Wan、CogVideoX、LTX-Video、HunyuanVideo

## Prompt

```text
A 5-second studio product video of a matte black wireless earbud case rotating slowly on a reflective dark glass surface. The camera stays fixed while the product completes a gentle quarter turn. Soft studio lights create clean highlights along the curved edges. Minimal dark gradient background, premium technology commercial style, realistic reflections, no logo, no text.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
warped product shape, extra objects, fast spinning, unstable rotation, flickering, fake text, logo, watermark, low quality, blurry
```

## Notes

- 产品视频要控制动作简单：慢旋转、推近、光线变化。
