# HunyuanVideo Environment Reveal

适用模型：HunyuanVideo、Wan T2V、CogVideoX

## Prompt

```text
A 6-second cinematic environment reveal of a futuristic research station built into a snowy mountain cliff. The camera begins close to a frosted metal railing, then slowly pulls back to reveal the full building glowing warmly against the cold landscape. Snow particles drift in the wind, distant mountains fade into mist, realistic scale, slow and stable camera movement, no people, no text.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
warped building, impossible perspective, chaotic camera, fast zoom, flickering lights, low quality, fake text, watermark, logo, cartoon style
```

## Notes

- 环境揭示镜头适合建筑、场景、产品发布片开头。
