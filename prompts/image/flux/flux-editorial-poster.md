# FLUX Editorial Poster

适用模型：FLUX.1、FLUX.2、其他自然语言友好模型

## Prompt

```text
An editorial poster-style image about artificial intelligence workflows. A transparent glass cube floats in the center, containing a miniature production line of glowing nodes connected by thin white lines. Around the cube are soft shadows, subtle reflections, and a calm dark gray studio background. The composition is minimal, balanced, and modern, with enough empty space at the top for a Chinese title to be added later. Soft cinematic lighting, high-end technology magazine style, no readable text, no logo.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
busy UI, random letters, fake Chinese text, neon overload, cyberpunk clutter, low resolution, watermark, logo, blurry, messy composition
```

## Notes

- FLUX 更适合完整自然语言，不需要堆 `masterpiece, best quality`。
- 中文标题建议后期手动加。
