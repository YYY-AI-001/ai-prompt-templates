# Wan I2V Single Action

## Prompt

```text
The woman in the reference image slowly raises her right hand to adjust her glasses, then returns to a relaxed pose. Her face, hairstyle, clothing, and body proportions remain unchanged. The camera stays at medium close-up with a very slight push-in. Soft indoor daylight, realistic motion, natural blinking, calm expression, no text, no logo.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
face changes, different clothing, extra fingers, broken hand, fast motion, multiple actions, body distortion, flickering, subtitles, watermark, logo, blurry
```

## Notes

- Wan I2V 更适合参考图 + 单动作。
