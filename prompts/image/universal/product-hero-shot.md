# Product Hero Shot

适用模型：FLUX、Qwen-Image、SDXL、Z-Image、HunyuanImage、ERNIE-Image

## Prompt

```text
A premium product hero shot of a matte black wireless earbud case placed on a reflective dark glass surface. The case is slightly open, revealing two earbuds inside. Soft studio lighting from the upper left creates clean highlights on the curved edges. Minimal background, subtle gradient from charcoal gray to deep blue, precise reflections, sharp focus, commercial product photography, 70mm lens look, high-end technology brand aesthetic, no text, no logo.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
messy background, cheap plastic look, scratches, fingerprints, extra objects, fake brand logo, unreadable text, distorted product shape, blurry, noisy, low quality, oversaturated colors
```

## Notes

- 产品图提示词要明确材质、摆放、光线、背景和商业用途。
- 不建议让模型生成真实品牌 logo。
