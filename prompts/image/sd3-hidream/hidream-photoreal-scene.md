# HiDream Photoreal Scene

适用模型：HiDream-I1 Full / Dev / Fast、其他高质量开源写实生图模型

## Prompt

```text
A realistic photo of a young industrial designer working late in a small studio. She sits beside a wooden desk covered with sketches, metal samples, a laptop, and a half-assembled desk lamp prototype. Warm desk light illuminates her face and hands, while cool blue city light enters through a large window behind her. Natural skin texture, believable hands, quiet concentration, documentary photography, 35mm lens, shallow depth of field, soft film grain, high detail, no text.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
plastic skin, doll face, extra fingers, fused fingers, distorted tools, fake text, over-sharpened, oversaturated, messy composition, watermark, logo
```

## Notes

- HiDream / DiT 类模型适合完整场景句，不要只堆风格词。
- 人物、环境、光线和道具要形成同一个叙事，不要互相抢主体。
