# Open-Sora Landscape Motion

适用模型：Open-Sora、轻量 T2V / I2V 开源视频模型

## Prompt

```text
A wide cinematic landscape shot of a mountain lake at dusk. Thin clouds move slowly across the sky, soft wind creates gentle ripples on the water, and warm sunset light reflects on the lake surface. The camera slowly pushes forward from the shore toward the center of the lake. Calm atmosphere, realistic motion, natural colors, no people, no text.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
fast camera shake, chaotic water, flickering sky, sudden scene change, low quality, blurry, text, watermark, logo
```

## Notes

- Open-Sora 类模型适合先用简单清楚的镜头测试运动稳定性。
- 风景类 prompt 优先写自然运动：云、水、风、光线变化。
