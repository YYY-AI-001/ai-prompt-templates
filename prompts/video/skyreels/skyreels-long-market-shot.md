# SkyReels Long Market Shot

适用模型：SkyReels V2、长视频开源模型、支持长镜头的 T2V / I2V 模型

## Prompt

```text
A continuous cinematic shot of a quiet morning street market slowly waking up. The camera moves forward at walking speed through a narrow lane, passing wooden fruit stalls, steam rising from breakfast pots, bicycles leaning against old walls, and shopkeepers opening fabric awnings. A young woman in a beige coat walks ahead of the camera, occasionally turning her head slightly but never stopping. Soft golden sunrise light, gentle mist, realistic human motion, stable perspective, documentary film atmosphere.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
scene cuts, sudden jump, changing main character, broken walking motion, flickering faces, unstable camera, warped stalls, random subtitles, watermark, logo
```

## Notes

- SkyReels 长镜头更需要稳定的主线动作，不适合在一个 prompt 里塞太多转场。
- 长视频建议用“连续镜头 + 主要人物 + 环境递进”的方式写。
