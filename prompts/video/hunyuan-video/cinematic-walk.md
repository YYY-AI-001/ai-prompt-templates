# HunyuanVideo Cinematic Walk

适用模型：HunyuanVideo、HunyuanVideo-I2V

## Prompt

```text
A cinematic 6-second shot of a young explorer walking through a misty bamboo forest at dawn. The camera follows from behind at shoulder height, slowly moving forward as the explorer steps along a narrow stone path. Soft morning light filters through the bamboo, light fog drifts across the path, clothing moves gently with each step, realistic motion, calm and mysterious atmosphere, no text.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
teleporting, sliding feet, distorted body, changing clothes, flickering, overexposed fog, static image, subtitles, watermark, logo, blurry
```

## Notes

- HunyuanVideo 适合电影感运动镜头，提示词要明确相机跟随方式和环境运动。
