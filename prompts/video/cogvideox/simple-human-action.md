# CogVideoX Simple Human Action

适用模型：CogVideoX、CogVideoX-I2V

## Prompt

```text
A 4-second medium shot of a man sitting at a wooden desk, gently opening a notebook and looking down to read the first page. Warm desk lamp light illuminates his hands and face. The camera remains still, realistic small movement, quiet study room atmosphere, natural hand motion, no text, no subtitles.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
extra hands, distorted fingers, fast action, page flickering, face changing, camera shake, blurry, watermark, subtitles, logo
```

## Notes

- CogVideoX 类模型更适合短、清晰、动作单一的 prompt。
