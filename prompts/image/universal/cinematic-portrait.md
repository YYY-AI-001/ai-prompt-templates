# Cinematic Portrait

适用模型：FLUX、Qwen-Image、SDXL、HunyuanImage、GLM-Image、ERNIE-Image、LongCat-Image、Z-Image

## Prompt

```text
A cinematic close-up portrait of a young Asian creative director standing near a large studio window during blue hour. Soft diffused light falls across the face, with subtle rim lighting outlining the shoulders. The subject wears a simple black jacket and a calm, focused expression. Shallow depth of field, 85mm lens look, realistic skin texture, natural pores, gentle film grain, muted teal and warm amber color grading, elegant editorial photography, clean background, high-end magazine portrait.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
cartoon, anime, plastic skin, over-smoothed face, distorted eyes, bad anatomy, extra fingers, blurry, low resolution, watermark, logo, fake text, overexposed, harsh flash
```

## Notes

- FLUX / Qwen 这类模型可以直接使用完整英文自然语言。
- SDXL 可拆成关键词结构，并保留 negative prompt。
- 如果需要固定人物，建议配合参考图或角色 LoRA。
