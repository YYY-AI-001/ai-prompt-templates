# Wan T2V Cozy Kitchen

适用模型：Wan T2V

## Prompt

```text
A 5-second warm cinematic shot of a cozy kitchen in the morning. A white cat carefully measures flour into a mixing bowl while a golden retriever gently stirs batter with a wooden spoon. Sunlight streams through the window, dust particles float in the air, the camera slowly pushes in from a medium shot. The motion is gentle and believable, charming but realistic, no text, no subtitles.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
extra animals, distorted paws, impossible motion, chaotic movement, flickering, blurry, subtitles, watermark, logo, low quality, messy background
```

## Notes

- T2V 可以更自由，但仍建议控制一个主要动作场景。
