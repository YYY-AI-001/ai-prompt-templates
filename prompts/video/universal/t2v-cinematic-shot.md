# Universal T2V Cinematic Shot

适用模型：Wan、HunyuanVideo、CogVideoX、LTX-Video、Mochi、其他开源视频模型

## Prompt

```text
A 5-second cinematic shot of a lone astronaut walking slowly across a quiet lunar surface. The camera tracks from left to right at a low angle, following the astronaut's footsteps. Fine moon dust rises gently with each step. Earth is visible in the distant black sky. Soft rim light outlines the spacesuit, realistic motion, calm and majestic atmosphere, no text, no logo.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
static image, frozen motion, fast chaotic movement, distorted body, extra limbs, flickering, warped helmet, blurry, low quality, subtitles, watermark, logo
```

## Notes

- 视频 prompt 要写动作、镜头、时长和场景。
- 不要一条 prompt 里写多个连续复杂动作。
