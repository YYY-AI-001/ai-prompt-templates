# Environment Concept

适用模型：FLUX、SDXL、HunyuanImage、GLM-Image、Wan T2I、Midjourney 类模型

## Prompt

```text
A wide cinematic concept image of a quiet mountain research station built into a cliffside above a sea of clouds. The architecture combines concrete, glass, and warm wooden interiors, with soft lights glowing through large panoramic windows. Early morning mist surrounds the structure, distant snow peaks in the background, calm atmosphere, realistic scale, detailed environment design, subtle human silhouettes for scale, natural color palette, cinematic landscape composition, 21:9 aspect ratio.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
fantasy castle, overly futuristic spaceship, cluttered details, unreadable text, bad perspective, distorted building, low quality, oversaturated, excessive fog, cartoon style
```

## Notes

- 适合概念图、视频关键帧、世界观设定。
- 如果模型容易跑偏，先减少“futuristic”等抽象词，增加具体材料和场景关系。
