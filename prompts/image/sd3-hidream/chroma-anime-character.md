# Chroma Anime Character

适用模型：Chroma、FLUX 派生模型、动漫 / 插画风格开源模型

## Prompt

```text
An original anime character standing on the rooftop of a quiet high school at sunset. She has short silver hair, clear blue eyes, a navy school jacket, and a red ribbon tie. A soft breeze moves her hair and skirt slightly. The sky is orange and pink, with distant city buildings below. Clean line art, expressive face, delicate shading, cinematic anime composition, warm rim light, high detail, no text, no logo.
```

## Negative Prompt

```text
low quality, bad anatomy, extra fingers, missing fingers, crossed eyes, messy hair, distorted uniform, duplicated character, watermark, signature, text
```

## Notes

- Chroma / FLUX 派生模型更吃“完整画面描述”，但动漫模型仍可保留 negative prompt。
- 如果要做角色设定集，可以把服装、发型、眼睛、道具固定成重复锚点。
